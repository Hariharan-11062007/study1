About our college :

PA College Of Engineering And Technology stands as a premier institution dedicated to academic excellence and holistic student development since its establishment in 1900. Affiliated with Anna university, our campus offers a dynamic learning environment featuring state-of-the-art infrastructure, including spacious smart classrooms, a digitally integrated library, and advanced computer laboratories. Our distinguished faculty, comprising industry experts and seasoned academicians, foster critical thinking and innovation through a rigorous curriculum balanced with vibrant extracurricular opportunities in sports, arts, and leadership clubs. Committed to nurturing global citizens, the college emphasizes personal growth, ethical values, and community service, ensuring every student graduates with the professional readiness and confidence to thrive in a competitive world.
